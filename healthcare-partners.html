How to Become a Partner

Our onboarding process ensures a smooth start to a long-term, trusted relationship.

  1. Initial Consultation: Submit your partnership inquiry and meet with our Malasakit Care advisors.
  2. Needs Assessment: We evaluate product requirements, logistics support, and compliance documentation.
  3. Proposal & Agreement: Tailored pricing, service levels, and support plans aligned with your goals.
  4. Onboarding & Training: System access, product orientation, and operational handover.
  5. Continuous Support: Regular business reviews, marketing programs, and service optimization.
