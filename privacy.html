Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

How Britton Distributions Inc. collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Introduction

Britton Distributions Inc. (“Britton”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to safeguarding the personal information of our customers, partners, patients, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information in accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, company, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Business information: partnership details, purchase history, and service requirements.
  • Online information: messages submitted through contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, event registrations, and website usage data.

3. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and process orders.
  • Manage partnerships, logistics arrangements, and regulatory documentation.
  • Send product updates, health tips, and marketing communications (with consent).
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and audit requirements.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with trusted third-party providers (such as logistics partners and IT service providers) solely for business operations. We ensure that these parties uphold confidentiality and data protection standards. Personal information may also be disclosed when required by law or government authorities.

5. Data Protection

Britton adopts organizational, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data against loss, unauthorized access, or misuse. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel who require the information to perform their duties.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Once data is no longer needed, it is securely disposed of or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent or object to processing activities, subject to applicable regulations. To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer using the details below.
