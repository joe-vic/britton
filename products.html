Quality Standards

Britton Distributions Inc. follows strict Good Distribution Practices (GDP) with FDA and DOH compliance across product handling stages. Each item is sourced from audited, GMP-certified manufacturing partners and validated throughout our logistics lifecycle.

  • Batch traceability and product authentication checks
  • Temperature monitoring for sensitive inventories
  • Documented product recall and pharmacovigilance processes
  • Regular quality audits and supplier performance reviews
