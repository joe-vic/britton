Our Story

Building Healthier Communities Since 2001

Britton Distributions Inc. began as a dedicated pharmaceutical distributor focused on providing reliable access to essential medicines. Our Journey

From Mandaluyong Roots to National Presence

Starting as a local distributor in 2001, Britton Distributions Inc. scaled operations through strong partner relationships, a commitment to regulatory compliance, and a culture anchored on Malasakit.

  • 2001: Founded with a small team serving Metro Manila pharmacies.
  • 2008: Expanded portfolio to include vitamins, supplements, and consumer health brands.
  • 2014: Established regional logistics hubs supporting Visayas and Mindanao partners.
  • 2022: Introduced Malasakit Care initiatives focused on preventive healthcare education.
