About Us

A Malasakit-driven pharmaceutical company enabling better healthcare access for Filipino families.

Company Background

Founded in 2001, Britton Distributions Inc. started as a local pharmaceutical distributor in Metro Manila. Through consistent service, reliable quality, and a Malasakit mindset, we have grown into a nationwide partner for pharmacies, hospitals, medical practitioners, and community health programs.

We continue to expand our portfolio of quality medicines and health essentials, delivering trusted brands that meet the evolving needs of Filipino households.

Experience

24 years of proven distribution expertise covering cold-chain logistics, retail merchandising, and institutional supply services.

Mission | Vision | Values

Malasakit guides every decision we make

We combine compassionate service with disciplined execution so lifesaving medicines and wellness products reach Filipino families when they need them most.

MISSION

Britton Distributions Inc. is committed to enhancing lives by providing Quality, Effective, and Affordable ( QEA ) medicines and consumer products, delivering solutions with excellence, compassion, and integrity.

  • Nationwide reach

    Temperature-controlled hubs and agile logistics enable access across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

  • Patient-first mindset

    Every program is designed around the needs of pharmacists, doctors, and Filipino families.

  • Partners in progress

    We co-create growth plans with suppliers and retailers to build resilient, long-term impact.

VISION

To be a valued national pharmaceutical company in the Philippines, providing exceptional healthcare and promoting wellness and happiness for Filipino families through the spirit of Malasakit

  • Innovation with purpose

    We introduce products and services that anticipate the evolving needs of patients and practitioners.

  • Trusted compliance

    A culture of integrity and transparency sustains our regulatory leadership and partner confidence.

  • Communities empowered

    Through Malasakit initiatives we help Filipinos live healthier, happier, and more hopeful lives.

Certifications & Compliance

Quality systems that safeguard every product handoff

Our regulatory, quality, and logistics teams collaborate to ensure every shipment is safe, traceable, and delivered with care.

FDA Licensed DOH Accredited ISO Aligned

We maintain digital documentation, continuous training, and proactive audits to stay ahead of regulatory reforms and protect the patients we serve.

  • Annual internal audits and partner assessments that reinforce good distribution practices.
  • GDP/GWP-compliant storage, cold-chain monitoring, and corrective action programs.
  • Serialized inventory and digital traceability from inbound inspection to last-mile delivery.

Regulatory Stewardship

Our FDA and DOH licenses are actively managed through scheduled renewals, policy reviews, and cross-functional compliance drills.

Quality Management

ISO-aligned SOPs govern product handling, cold-chain integrity, CAPA reviews, and continuous improvement initiatives.

Certified Partnerships

We collaborate exclusively with GMP and PIC/S certified manufacturers and perform routine supplier audits to secure the supply chain.

Digital Traceability

Integrated systems provide real-time visibility, documentation, and recall readiness for every lot we manage.
