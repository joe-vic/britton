Logistics Excellence

Distribution Network

Nationwide coverage supported by reliable warehousing, transport, and partner coordination.

Coverage Areas

Britton Distributions Inc. services partners across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Our distribution model combines central hubs with satellite depots to ensure timely and compliant deliveries.

Metro Manila & Luzon

Primary distribution centers in Pasig and San Juan with daily runs to North and South Luzon retailers, hospitals, and institutional accounts.

  • Central Luzon & Ilocos routes
  • CALABARZON & MIMAROPA deliveries
  • Bicol Region partner support

Visayas

Cebu hub managing shipments to Western, Central, and Eastern Visayas, including island and coastal delivery routes.

  • Cebu City, Mandaue, and Lapu-Lapu coverage
  • Iloilo, Bacolod, and Dumaguete partner visits
  • Bohol and Leyte scheduled deliveries

Mindanao

Davao satellite warehouse supporting partners in Northern, Southern, and Western Mindanao with coordinated logistics.

  • Davao City, Cagayan de Oro, and General Santos
  • Zamboanga Peninsula and Caraga deliveries
  • Cold-chain distribution for sensitive products
